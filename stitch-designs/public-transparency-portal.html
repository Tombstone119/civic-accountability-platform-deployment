About This Contract

This contract covers the supply and delivery of advanced medical equipment including ventilators, patient monitoring systems, and diagnostic imaging machines to public hospitals nationwide. The procurement aims to modernize ICU units across 15 provincial healthcare facilities to ensure equitable access to high-quality care.

Quality assurance protocols are built into the delivery schedule, requiring independent verification of equipment specifications upon arrival at each destination site.
